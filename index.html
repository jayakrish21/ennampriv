Privacy Policy

Ennam  ·  Last updated: May 2026

The short version: Ennam stores everything on your device only. We do not collect, transmit, or share any of your data. We have no servers, no analytics, no ads, and no accounts (in the free version).

1. What data we collect

Ennam collects no personal data from you. The app works entirely on your device. All thoughts, decisions, reflections, and insights you record are stored locally in your device's storage and nowhere else.

2. How your data is stored

All data is encrypted on your device using AES-256-GCM encryption before it is written to local storage. A unique encryption key is generated for your device and never leaves it. This means:

  • Your thoughts are unreadable to anyone without access to your device
  • We (the developers) cannot read your data — it never reaches us
  • Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all your data

3. Third-party services

The free version of Ennam uses no third-party services, SDKs, analytics tools, or advertising networks. There is nothing phoning home.

4. Children's privacy

Ennam is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

5. Data export and deletion

You can export all your data at any time from Settings → Export all decisions. To delete your data permanently, simply uninstall the app. All locally stored data is removed when the app is uninstalled.

6. Changes to this policy

If we ever introduce features that change how data is handled (such as optional cloud sync), this policy will be updated and you will be notified within the app before any change takes effect. Your data will never be uploaded without your explicit opt-in.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please reach out at vikisocialcn@gmail.com.
