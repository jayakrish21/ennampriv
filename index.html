About Ennam

Ennam is a private decision journal that helps you think through important choices, spot blind spots, and reflect on outcomes over time.

Common Questions

Do I need an account?

No. Ennam works without an account. Your decisions and reflections are stored locally on your device by default.

Where is my data stored?

Your decision data is stored on your device. Ennam is designed to be local-first and private by default.

How do I export my data?

Open Ennam, go to Settings → Export all data, and save the JSON backup.

How do I delete my data?

Open Ennam, go to Settings → Wipe all data. This permanently deletes local decisions, reflections, settings, and the local encryption key from the device.

Does Ennam use AI?

AI Coach is optional and off by default. If you enable it, Ennam sends a brief decision summary to generate tailored reflection questions. You can turn AI Coach on or off in Settings at any time.

Why are reflection reminders not appearing?

Make sure notifications are enabled for Ennam in your device Settings. You can also check Ennam’s Settings screen to enable reflection reminders.
